1-16-2014
Hello everyone!

Last night we had way too much fun playing at Metropolitan Lounge in downtown Annapolis. Every Wednesday night, they host an open mic which we will be trying to attend as often as possible. We met some really great musicians and new friends, and hope to see some of you come out to see us and all of the great talent we have here in Annapolis.

In other news, we have uploaded a new song to our page! It's called "Psalms". We recorded a demo of it in Delaware with some friends, and have begun working on a full band version of it which will be added to our live sets, as well as a few other new songs, too.

-Daniel
